Our Mission

To create a sustainable ecosystem for electronic waste management by connecting consumers, collectors, and recyclers through technology.

EPS (E-Waste Pickup System) is a digital platform developed by students of L.J. College of Computer Applications to address the growing problem of electronic waste in urban areas.

Our Vision

A world where every electronic device is responsibly recycled
